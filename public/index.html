Meet Your Coach

Riya Sharma, founder of AquaCoach, is a certified swim coach with over a decade of experience. A former national-level swimmer, Riya has dedicated her career to helping swimmers of all ages and abilities reach their full potential. Her coaching style combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of each athlete's unique needs and goals.
Her coaching philosophy focuses on creating a supportive environment where every swimmer can progress at their own pace while still being challenged to improve. Through structured training, technical guidance, and personalized attention, Riya helps students develop stronger endurance, better stroke mechanics, and long-term confidence both inside and outside the pool. Known for her calm teaching style and attention to detail, she believes that swimming is more than just a sport — it is a life skill that builds focus, resilience, and self-belief.
